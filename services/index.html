Our Services

Payer And Provider Credentialing

We have experts who have hands on experience in enrolling the provider with major federal and commercial insurance. We keep ourselves updated by CMS and AMA on any changes in the fee schedule that requires to be modified in our billing. We make effective enrollment state wise through various pay and effective channels. Thus, reducing major chunks of claims denials and improving billing process.

A/R Follow up

Our team are well trained to work offline using spreadsheets, reports or directly generating reports from the client’s software. Our Account receivables team are experts in handling the various insurance categories/plans like HMO, PPO, Commercials, workers compensation, Medicare, Medicaid and Following up aggressively for denied or outstanding claims.Our team strives to decrease the AR days and to improve the revenue.

Medical Billing Services

Medical Billing Services Appointment Scheduling – Eligibility Verification – Demographics Entry – Charge Entry – Payment Posting – Denial Posting We always provide 99% accuracy and 12-15 hrs of “Turn-around time” (TAT) of each and every transaction that is delivered to our clients. Our data team has the ability to work on scanned images as well as electronically submitted charge claims.
